How was built?

Spotify API is the main component of this application. The search is based on Spotify library of Artists and Musics, returning the respective Artist, Music, Cover Image and Music Metadata. Let's dive in to understand some of the music metadata parameters available:

  • Danceability: describes how suitable a track is for dancing based on a combination of musical elements including tempo, rhythm stability, beat strength, and overall regularity. A value of 0.0 is least danceable and 1.0 is most danceable.
  • Energy: represents a perceptual measure of intensity and activity. Typically, energetic tracks feel fast, loud, and noisy.
  • Acousticness: describes how acoustic a song is. A score of 1.0 means the song is most likely to be an acoustic one.
  • Valence: A measure from 0.0 to 1.0 describing the musical positiveness conveyed by a track. Tracks with high valence sound more positive (e.g. happy, cheerful, euphoric), while tracks with low valence sound more negative (e.g. sad, depressed, angry).

In addition to it, I've introduced MusixMatch API, allowing the application to return the lyric of the respective music. Unfortunately neither Spotify nor MusixMatch API provide the language of a track. To figure it out, I've utilized langdetect a language-detection algorithm library.
